Hello,

You probably came here looking for an RP from the old version of RPNow. This old version of RPNow was shut down in July 2019, as was announced in November 2018 in this post. If you did not download your RP between that time, it is unfortunately no longer accessible.

The good news is that RPNow is back, and you can use it again, and create new RP's. It works somewhat differently now: instead of saving bookmarked links to your stories, you now can view all of the RP's you have created on the homepage of the site. As a consequence of this, the site can no longer be used without creating a login (although we still do not require any personal information from you.)

Go back to the homepage and enjoy using the site.

Have a nice day.
